Addressc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/italia/onto/CLV/Address

This class represents an "addressable object" or "addressable representation". In most situations the addressable objects are current, real world objects. In the Italian context it represents the external access from a circulation area to a simple unit such as dwellings, offices, premises where economic activities are located, etc.
